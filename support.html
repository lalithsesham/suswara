FAQ

  • How does Suswara work?
    Suswara uses your device's microphone to detect the pitch you sing or play, and maps it to the closest Carnatic swara.
  • Why do I need to allow microphone access?
    The app requires microphone access to listen to your voice or instrument and identify the note.
  • Is my audio data stored?
    No, your audio is processed in real-time on your device and is not stored or sent anywhere. See our Privacy Policy for details.
