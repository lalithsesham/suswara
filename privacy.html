Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 25, 2025

Suswara respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS app.

Information We Collect

  • Microphone Access: The app uses your device's microphone to detect musical notes. Audio is processed in real-time on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Third-Party Services

Suswara does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services.

Children's Privacy

Suswara does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@suswara.app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.
